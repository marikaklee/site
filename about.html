Manuel Lam, MD
Let food be thy medicine and medicine be thy food." -Hippocrates

Dr. Manuel Lam holds a Medical Degree and Masters in Medical Sciences from Brown's Alpert Medical School. He trained at Stanford Hospital & Clinics, and co-founded Journal Club App during his residency, which provides concise summaries of major landmark journal articles to health care professionals.

As well as working as a hospitalist in Palo Alto, Dr. Lam is a board-certified internist who sees patients privately in Menlo Park for obesity, diabetes, metabolic syndromes. His medical approach integrates conventional medicine, nutritional science, and metabolic laboratory diagnostics to find the root cause of disease and illness. He has a keen interest in using tools such as continuous glucose monitors as biofeedback to understand the impact of food, exercise, mindset and sleep on our blood sugars. He utilizes nutritional strategies, such as intermittent fasting and low carbohydrate or ketogenic diets, to treat chronic disease as well as to optimize metabolic health, cognitive performance and longevity in the healthy.

When I'm not working, I enjoy reading and drinking coffee at Philz coffee, playing tennis, practicing piano, cuddling with my cat Mimi, and oh yeah, spending time with my fiancee Marika Lee.
