Manuel Lam, MD
Let food be thy medicine and medicine be thy food." -Hippocrates

Dr. Manuel Lam holds a Medical Degree and Masters in Medical Sciences from Brown's Alpert Medical School. He trained at Stanford Hospital and Clinics, and co-founded Journal Club App during his residency, which provides concise summaries of major landmark journal articles to health care professionals.

As well as working as a hospitalist in Palo Alto, Dr. Lam sees patients privately in Menlo Park. His medical approach integrates conventional medicine, nutritional science, and metabolic diagnostics to find the root cause of disease and illness. Particularly, he has a keen interest in using tools such as low carbohydrate or ketogenic diets and intermittent fasting to prevent and treat chronic disease, as well as optimizing one’s health and life span.
