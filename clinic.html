Services

Your initial consultation will last 45-60 minutes so that Dr. Lam can formulate a personalized, prescription for treatment and weight loss, customized and tailored specifically to your needs. An initial consultation visit includes:

• A detailed review of your medical and weight history
• A detailed review of your medications, identifying drugs that may be negatively impacting your weight, as well as An anti-diabetic and hypertension meds, that could put you at risk during a fast.
• An analysis of your nutrition, exercise, sleep and stress
• Laboratory studies to monitor your metabolism
• Microbiome testing to help you understand the potential role of your gut health
• Resting metabolic rate testing (if indicated)
• Body composition analysis to assess and track your body fat percentage (if indicated)
• Blood glucose and ketone testing (if indicated)
• Please complete the New Patient Form (preferably one week in advance) so that we may extensively review your information and prepare for your visit.

NEW PATIENT FORM

We appreciate your busy schedules and respect your time. Traditionally, our follow-up visits are scheduled on a weekly to monthly basis during the active treatment and weight loss period directly with Dr. Lam. The frequency of follow up visits may be customized to be less frequent (for flexibility) or to be more often (for more accountability and intensity, such as in diabetics on hypoglycemic agents or insulin). Once you have reached your goal weight, we will focus on weight maintenance and further space out your follow up visits. We may safely adjust or remove current medications you are taking for diabetes or blood pressure. Review progress in nutrition, exercise, sleep and stress; feedingand fasting schedules laboratory studies to monitor your metabolism (if indicated) Review microbiome testing blood glucose and ketone testing (if indicated) Ask about continuous glucose monitoring as biofeedback to understand the impact of food, exercise, mindset and sleep on your blood sugars

We do not participate in any insurance plans. However, if losing weight is a treatment for a medically diagnosed disease (such as obesity, diabetes, or hypertension), your visits may be qualified medical expenses and tax-deductible. Consider using your Health Savings Account or Flexible Spending Account. Otherwise, consider itemizing your deductions on Schedule A. You can use your regular insurance for microbiome collection kits and laboratory services (any Quest or Labcorp of your choice). Check with your tax accountant.
