Mission

The causes of obesity and weight gain go far beyond the oversimplified paradigm of simply eating too much and moving too little. We understand how hard it is to lose weight, and we know that it is complicated and multi-factorial. There are many dozens of factors beyond simple diet and exercise that predispose, cause, and contribute to weight gain. Some are internal, including genetic predisposition, hormonal imbalances, insulin resistance, and metabolism; and some are external, including medical conditions, medications, stress, sleep deprivation, cultural beliefs, and social influences. As a result, there is no one-size-fits-all intervention that will work for all people to achieve successful long-term weight loss.

Dr. Manuel Lam investigates and treats a wide range of weight-related medical conditions, including obesity, metabolic syndrome, diabetes, and high blood pressure. He helps patients reduce their weight, lower their blood pressure and reverse their type 2 diabetes, through nutritional and lifestyle strategies, while getting them off their prescription medications.

Services

Your initial consultation will last 45-60 minutes so that Dr. Lam can take a full, detailed history as well as come up with an appropriate personalized, medically supervised weight loss and treatment plan, customized and tailored specifically to your needs. A comprehensive medical evaluation includes:

A detailed review of your medical and weight history
A detailed review of your medications, identifying drugs that may be negatively impacting your weight
An analysis of your nutrition and exercise status
An analysis of habits, strategies, and other factors potentially contributing to weight gain
Laboratory studies
A body scan, assessing your body fat percentage
A sequencing-based microbiome screening test, providing detailed and accurate information to help you understand your gut health
Continuous glucose monitoring to understand how food, exercise, stress and sleep affect your blood sugars (if indicated)
Blood glucose and ketone testing (if indicated)

Prior to your first visit, please complete the NEW PATIENT FORMS and if possible send them one week in advance so that we can extensively review your information and prepare prior to your visit. We appreciate your busy schedules and respect your time. Traditionally, our follow-up visits are scheduled on a once monthly basis during the active weight loss period with Dr. Lam. We may adjust or remove current medications you may be taking for diabetes, blood pressure or cholesterol. The frequency of follow up visits may be customized to be less frequent (for more flexibility) or to be more often (for more accountability and intensity, but particularly for diabetics on hypoglycemic agents or insulin). Once you have reached your goal weight, we will focus on weight maintenance and space your follow up visits out further.
