Manuel Lam, MD
Let food be thy medicine and medicine be thy food." -Hippocrates

Dr. Manuel Lam investigates for and treats metabolic syndrome and insulin resistance. This includes a wide range of weight-related metabolic disorders, including obesity, prediabetes, diabetes, high blood pressure, high cholesterol, fatty liver, polycystic ovarian syndrome, Alzheimer’s, and cancer. Progression to diabetes increases your risk of heart disease, stroke, peripheral vascular disease including amputations, kidney disease requiring dialysis, and blindness.
We understand that obesity and metabolic syndrome goes far beyond the oversimplified paradigm of simply eating too much and moving too little. We understand that it is complicated, and we seek to shed light on the unique challenges that each individual faces, including genetics, hormonal imbalances, gut microbiome, medications, stress, sleep, cultural and social influences. As a result, there is no one-size-fits-all intervention that will work for all people to achieve successful long-term weight loss.
Our program’s philosophy centers around nutritional and lifestyle strategies that aim to lower insulin, reverse insulin resistance, and lose body fat. These strategies include personalized intermittent fasting protocols, education regarding eating whole natural nutrient dense foods, avoiding added sugars and refined grains, and increasing natural fats. Dr. Lam provides contextual medical advice regarding pre-existing medical conditions, medications, and metabolic laboratory testing. Under his medical supervision, patients reduce their weight, lower their blood pressure and reverse their type 2 diabetes, while getting them off their prescription medications.
